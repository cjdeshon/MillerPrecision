About Us

Miller Precision was founded in Fall 2024 by Bradley Miller, who identified a growing demand for high-quality fleet and trailer cleaning services while working in the construction industry. Recognizing both the need and his passion for the work, he launched the company with a commitment to professionalism and reliability. In Spring 2025, Antonio Gatewood joined as a partner to support the company’s rapid growth and ensure every client receives the highest level of service.
